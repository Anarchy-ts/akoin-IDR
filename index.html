About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital risk, cybersecurity and technology governance roles. We bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical capability, producing professionals who are ready to operate from day one.

Through deep collaboration between universities and industry partners, IDR prepares talent for work in regulated, high-consequence environments — from financial services and government to critical national infrastructure — where digital risk management is not optional, but essential.
